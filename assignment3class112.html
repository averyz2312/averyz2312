Best spaghetti recipe.

Ingredients

1 pound ground beef

1 large onion, chopped

2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce

1 garlic clove, minced

1 bay leaf

1 tablespoon minced fresh basil

2 teaspoons minced fresh oregano

2 teaspoon sugar

1/2 teaspoon pepper

Hot cooked spaghetti

Fresh oregano, optional

Preparing

    Cook ground beef and onion until meat is no longer pink and onion is tender; drain. Add the next eight ingredients; bring to a boil.

    Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Remove the bay leaf. Serve with spaghetti.
